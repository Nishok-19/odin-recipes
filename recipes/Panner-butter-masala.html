Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Butter
  • Cashew nuts
  • Cloves
  • Bay leaves
  • Masalas
    • Chilli powder
    • Corriondor powder
    • Garam masala
    • Salt
    • Sugar
  • Kasuri methi
  • Panner
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Oil
  • Cream

Steps

Follow these steps carefully ;

  1. Take a pan and heat it with some butter and oil in heat.
  2. Once it is heated a bit, Add the spices such as cloves and bay leaves and leave it for a moment.
  3. Now add the onions that we cut earlier into the pan and sortee it untill it turns nice and transparent.
  4. Take the Tomatoes and blend them using a blender and add it into the pan after adding Ginger-Garlic paste, Don't forget to close the lid or it could make a mess in you kitchen.
  5. Add the Cashew-nuts after soaking them in hot water for about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Cool this mixture and blend it.
  7. Take the pan we used earlier and add some butter and oil in it.
  8. Add some finely chopped onions to it and sortee it untill it turns mushy and transparent.
  9. Add all the magic masalas we took earlier and sortee it under low-flame for a minute.
  10. Add the blended base gravy here and close the lid, as you are about to enter battlefield where there are going to be a lot of explosions ahead :)
  11. Add water and panner, cook it for 5-10 minutes then add Kasuri methi.
  12. Taadaa! You just gave birth to an awesome little dish that can spread happiness and joyy.
  13. Make sure to do it in excess.
