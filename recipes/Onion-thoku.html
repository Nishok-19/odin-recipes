Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Masalas
    • Chilli powder
    • Corriondor powder
    • Garam masala
    • Salt
  • Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Oil

Steps

Follow these steps carefully ;

  1. Take a pan and heat it with oil in heat.
  2. Once it is heated a bit add the onions that we cut earlier into the pan and sortee it untill it turns golden brown.
  3. Take the Tomatoes and chop them finely,add it into the pan after adding Ginger-Garlic paste
  4. Add all the magic masalas we took earlier and sortee it under low-flame for a minute.
  5. Taadaa! You just gave birth to an awesome little dish that can spread happiness and joyy.
  6. Make sure to do it in excess.
