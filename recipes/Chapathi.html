Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

Follow these steps carefully ;

  1. Take a pan and pour all the wheat flour.
  2. Add some oil and salt to it.
  3. Now slowly add milk to it while kneading it.
  4. Once it has become soft keep it aside at least for 15 minutes and take a tawa to make chapathis to the shape you prefer.
  5. Taadaa! You just gave birth to an awesome little dish that can spread happiness and joyy.
  6. Make sure to do it in excess.
